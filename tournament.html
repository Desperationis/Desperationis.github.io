Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Tournament

Info / Rules

Admission fee is $2 and the tournament will be held during snack and lunch in room ##. Only 32 people can sign up due to time constraints. If you have any questions regarding the tournament, please ask our staff - Diego Contreras (owner of this website), Joshua Amaro, and Efrain Escobes.

  • No refunds after signup.

  • No bets or gambling.

  • No grade discrimination or raging.

  • No foul language or screaming.

  • Food and beverages be stayed away from console and controllers. It is required you wash your hands before playing if you are eating something.

  • Leave adequate space to move around.

  • Controllers / systems must remain in the classroom.

  • Ask our staff beforehand for custom controllers.

  • People playing their match shouldn't be ditracted.
If basic rules are not followed after a warning, you will be kicked out of the tournament.

Game Settings

  • No Mii characters.

  • No items.

  • No Final Smashes.

  • 3-stock battle

  • Only Omega and Battlefield stage types are allowed.

  • First map is chosen by P1. The map following that will be choosen by P2.

  • The only DLC character allowed is Piranha Plant.
You can bring your own controller.

Match Info

All matches are going to be best out of 3. If you are not present when your match is up, then your opponent will be the default winner. If neither of you show up, then anyone facing the winner of that match will be declared the winner. This is also not a exception for getting a refund. Game rules are as so:

  • Glitches are not allowed.

  • No Pausing or Quitting during the match.

  • If the game ends with a tie and leads to sudden death, then there will be a match reset with 1 stock. This is because range characters would easily win sudden death.
