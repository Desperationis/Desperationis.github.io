Guys

A cellular automaton... sorta. Written in C++ and SDL, you can summon moving square blocks on the field for them to eat food, fight, inbreed, and die. As they summon with random speed and range, only some with a combination of the two will be able to take over the world. Adjusting these parameters are easy, thanks to the sexy, handcrafted UI.
